🛰️ Safety-Sync

The "Don't-Forget-About-Me" Safety System.
❓ What is it?

Safety-Sync is made for students who walk alone at night. Most apps make you press a "Help" button during a scary situation. Safety-Sync is different. You tell the app: "I should be home in 20 minutes." If you don't enter your secret PIN within 20 minutes, the app automatically tells security that something is wrong.
✨ Main Features

    Automatic Help: If your timer hits zero, the alarm starts by itself.

    Live Tracking: Your location and phone battery are sent to a security dashboard every 30 seconds.

    Security PIN: Only you can stop the timer by entering your private 4-digit code.

    Emergency Mode: The screen flashes red and a loud siren plays if the time runs out.

    Admin View: A special page for guards to see exactly where you are on Google Maps.

🛠️ How it works

    Start: Enter your name, email, and how long your walk will take.

    Walk: Keep the app open. It sends your location to the cloud.

    Finish: Reach your hostel, enter your PIN, and the journey ends safely.

    Emergency: If you don't finish on time, the "Admin" gets a loud alert with your map location.

🚀 How to use it

    Open the index.html file in any browser.

    Set a 4-digit PIN in the Settings.

    Enter your journey details and hit Start.
